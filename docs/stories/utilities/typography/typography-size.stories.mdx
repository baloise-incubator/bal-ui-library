<Lead>Diverse color palettes available for the text of an element.</Lead>

## Classes

The classes `has-text-xxx` defines the font-color. For more colors have a look a the [Font Color Tokens]().

<CssTypographySize />

## Examples

Light is only available for font family title.

### Text Font Family

<Code language="html" code={`
<p>Regular - Lorem ipsum dolor sit, amet consectetur adipisicing elit.</p>
<p class="has-text-weight-light">Light - Lorem ipsum dolor sit, amet consectetur adipisicing elit.</p>
<p class="has-text-weight-bold">Bold - Lorem ipsum dolor sit, amet consectetur adipisicing elit.</p>
`} />

### Title Font Family

<Code language="html" code={`
<p class="is-family-title">Regular - Lorem ipsum dolor sit, amet consectetur adipisicing elit.</p>
<p class="is-family-title has-text-weight-light">Light - Lorem ipsum dolor sit, amet consectetur adipisicing elit.</p>
<p class="is-family-title has-text-weight-bold">Bold - Lorem ipsum dolor sit, amet consectetur adipisicing elit.</p>
`} />
