## Wrap

Flexible items will wrap if needed.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="is-flex is-flex-wrap-wrap" style="max-width: 500px">
  <div class="is-flex is-align-items-center is-justify-content-center has-background-green-3 m-normal has-radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 100px">1</div>
  <div class="is-flex is-align-items-center is-justify-content-center has-background-green-3 m-normal has-radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 100px">2</div>
  <div class="is-flex is-align-items-center is-justify-content-center has-background-green-3 m-normal has-radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 100px">3</div>
</div>
`}
/>

## Wrap-Reverse

Flexible items will wrap if needed, but in reverse order.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="is-flex is-flex-wrap-wrap-reverse" style="max-width: 500px">
  <div class="is-flex is-align-items-center is-justify-content-center has-background-green-3 m-normal has-radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 100px">1</div>
  <div class="is-flex is-align-items-center is-justify-content-center has-background-green-3 m-normal has-radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 100px">2</div>
  <div class="is-flex is-align-items-center is-justify-content-center has-background-green-3 m-normal has-radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 100px">3</div>
</div>
`}
/>

## Nowrap

Flexible items maintain their position without wrapping.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`

  <div class="is-flex is-align-items-center is-justify-content-center has-background-green-3 m-normal has-radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 100px">1</div>

</div>
`}
