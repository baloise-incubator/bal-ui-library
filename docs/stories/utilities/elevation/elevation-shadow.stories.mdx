## Box Shadow

The default shadow for cards is the normal one. Reserve the large shadow, for instance, for hover effects.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="is-flex is-align-items-center is-justify-content-center fg-xx-large">
    <div class="has-shadow-normal is-flex is-align-items-center is-justify-content-center has-background-green-3 has-radius-normal p-normal">shadow-normal</div>
    <div class="has-shadow-large is-flex is-align-items-center is-justify-content-center has-background-green-3 has-radius-normal p-normal">shadow-large</div>
</div>
`}
/>

## Text Shadow

Text with a light shadow to increase readability on images.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<p class="has-text-shadow">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consetetur sadipscing elitr, sed diam nonumy eirmod tempor invidunt ut labore et dolore magna aliquyam erat, sed diam voluptua.</p>
`}
/>
